Top Five Books

American Gods is about a man caught between a coming war between the old gods and the new.


Grimnoir Chronicles is a historic fiction series set in the 1930s detailing a world where people are born with magical abilities.


Pride and Prejudice is a surprisingly dramatic love story fit to rival any present day dramas.


John Dies at the End is a trippy sarcastic fantasy book about monsters and young adults who make terrible decisions. It's great.


The lessons learned in this book stay with you for a lifetime. I think everyone should read it at least once.
